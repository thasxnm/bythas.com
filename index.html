I am Thasneem

Aspiring Data Analyst

Creativity is often stereotyped as belonging only to the arts, often defined as the expression of ideas and emotions through a physical medium like painting or music. However, as someone from a STEM background, I’d like to debunk that notion.

This website is my canvas — a way to express who I am and my interests, brought to life through code. It represents my interests in a visual and interactive way.

As a practicing data analyst, I also find creativity in data. From data curation to storytelling through dashboards and visualizations, data becomes a medium of expression — one that drives impactful decisions.
