Dashboards for Quality Control in Chemical Manufacturing

Dashboard Development: Developed data visualization dashboards on Oracle Analytics Cloud to monitor and present production performance metrics and quality indicators.

Sentiment Analysis Integration: Integrated a pre-trained Sentiment Analysis Model from OCI Language into the dashboard to assess customer feedback from surveys and uncover insights on product satisfaction/dissatisfaction in the product and production process.

The aim was to reduce reliance on manual inspections and improve quality control and accuracy. The process of implementing the model included: Data labelling, Training, and Deployment.
